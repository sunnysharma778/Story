Chapter 4: A Divine Encounter

One evening, as Ravi sat in deep meditation, he felt an overwhelming sense of peace and joy enveloping him. To his astonishment, the divine form of Shree Radhe appeared before him in a radiant vision. Her eyes, filled with compassion, gazed into Ravi's soul, and a voice, soft as the gentle breeze, spoke words of guidance and encouragement.

Shree Radhe revealed that Ravi's devotion had touched her divine heart, and she bestowed upon him a sacred mission. Ravi was to share the teachings of love and compassion with the world, serving as a conduit for the divine message of Shree Radhe. Filled with gratitude and humility, Ravi embraced this divine calling, dedicating himself to the service of humanity with unwavering love.
